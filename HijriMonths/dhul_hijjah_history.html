  • 18 Dhul Hijjah 35 AH: Martyrdom of the third caliph of the Muslims, Sayyidunā ʿUthmān ibn ʿAffān (r)

  • Dhul Hijjah 44 AH: Passing away of Sayyidunā ʿAbd Allah ibn Qays Abū Mūsā al-Ashʿarī (r)
